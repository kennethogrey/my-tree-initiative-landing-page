Friends of My Tree Initiative

Welcome to the Friends of My Tree Donors Group, where your annual membership donations play a pivotal role in supporting our operational expenses. By becoming an annual member, you become an integral part of our mission to protect and preserve trees. Your contributions directly fuel our efforts to make the world greener. In return, enjoy a range of exclusive benefits that deepen your connection to nature and our community. Join us today and make a lasting impact while enjoying the rewards of being a Friend of My Tree.

Dr. Karusa Kiragu
Director of Change Management at the UNAIDS

The backbone of My Tree initiative and the cornerstone of My Tree donor history. Her unwavering commitment to our cause has made her the organization's most significant individual donor. Driven by her passion for environmental conservation, she has consistently supported My Tree's initiatives, providing vital resources that have allowed us to plant trees, run educational programs, and make a lasting impact on our planet. Beyond her generous financial contributions, Dr. Kiragu's mentorship and invaluable introductions to influential partners have propelled My Tree Initiative to new heights. She is, without a doubt, our biggest individual donor and the driving force behind our mission's success. My Tree owes its existence and impact to Dr. Karusa Kiragu, our biggest donor and guiding light.

Gen. Katumba Wamala
Minister of works & Transport

General Edward Katumba Wamala stands as the unwavering champion of My Tree Initiative, with a donation history that reflects his profound commitment to our cause. His generous and consistent contributions have been the lifeblood of our projects, enabling us to make a significant impact on the environment. General Wamala's belief in the abilities of our young leaders to effect positive change in our environment is truly inspiring. Beyond his financial support, he provides invaluable guidance, steering us towards impactful initiatives. Moreover, his extensive network has introduced My Tree Initiative to a broader community of supporters in government and the private sector, amplifying our mission's reach. His dedication is a testament to the positive change we can create together.

Professor Maggie Kigozi
Director Zuri Model Farm

Professor Maggie Kigozi, the public face and shining star of My Tree Initiative, has not only been our biggest promoter but also a philanthropic powerhouse, contributing not only financial support but also her unwavering belief in our mission. Professor Kigozi's contributions have fueled our growth, allowing us to plant thousands of trees and make a significant impact on the environment. As the face of My Tree Initiative in the public eye, she has brought attention and credibility to our cause. Her regular guidance to our young leaders is invaluable, shaping us into a more effective and passionate organization. Professor Maggie Kigozi is not just a donor; she's the heartbeat of our initiative.

Dr. Antonio Querido
Country Director FAO Uganda

Dr. Antonio Querido's contribution to My Tree Initiative extends far beyond financial support. His generous donations have played a vital role in driving our environmental projects. However, Dr. Querido's true value lies in his wealth of knowledge and expertise. He serves as the knowledge powerhouse for our young leaders, sharing his managerial experience and professional insights on how to structure and run the organization effectively. His guidance has been instrumental in shaping My Tree into a professionally managed entity. Furthermore, Dr. Querido's expertise in securing institutional grants has enabled us to access critical funding, ensuring our mission's sustainability. We are deeply grateful for his multifaceted support, which continues to fortify our foundation for a greener future.
